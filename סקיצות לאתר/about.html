Firewall is a service provided by the device or dedicated program that is one of the computer’s security against burglary carried out by hackers. The purpose of a firewall is to filter incoming and outgoing data to a computer over a network or the Internet. Frequently firewall is a dedicated program that the user installs the operating system. Sometimes also the service that you can activate your Internet Service Provider for a small fee, or part of the network infrastructure – separate computer, network module or component of the router.
